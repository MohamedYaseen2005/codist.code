ACHIEVEMENTS


The Student of the Month award recognizes outstanding students who demonstrate exceptional dedication, progress, and enthusiasm in learning coding skills within our app. Eligibility Criteria To be eligible for the

of the Month award, students must: Be an active user: Students must have a valid account and be actively using the app. Show consistent progress: Students must demonstrate consistent progress in

completing lessons, exercises, and projects. Achieve milestones: Students must achieve specific milestones, such as completing a certain number of lessons or achieving a high score on a coding challenge.

Students must participate in online discussions, forums, or other community activities within the app. Selection Process The Student of the Month will be selected by our team based on the following criteria:

Progress and achievement: Students who demonstrate significant progress and achievement in their coding skills. Consistency and dedication: Students who consistently log in and engage with the app.

Community involvement: Students who actively participate in online discussions and community activities. Code quality and creativity: Students who demonstrate exceptional coding skills, creativity, and problem-

solving abilities.
